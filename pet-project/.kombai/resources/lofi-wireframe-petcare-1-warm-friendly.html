🎨 Design Highlights

  • Color Palette: Warm oranges (#FFB347, #FF9970), soft reds (#FF6B6B), golden yellows (#FFD93D)
  • Typography: Playful Comic Sans MS for friendly feel, strong visual hierarchy
  • Spacing: Generous padding (24px) for comfort and breathing room
  • Components: Rounded corners (16-20px), soft shadows, emotional color accents
  • Interaction: Hover animations (translateY), card elevation on hover
